Executive Summary

Project Overview

Establishment of a specialized micro drone manufacturing plant in Spain, producing advanced LiDAR-equipped micro drones for EU defense applications by repurposing existing electronics production lines.

Key Value Proposition

  • Enhanced surveillance and reconnaissance capabilities for EU military operators
  • European sovereignty in critical defense technology
  • Rapid deployment and operational flexibility in diverse mission scenarios
  • Cost-effective alternative to larger UAV platforms

Implementation Timeline

2-3 year phased implementation approach with production beginning within 18-21 months.

Investment Required

€2.5-5 million initial investment with projected ROI of 120-150% over 5 years.

Micro Drone Concept

Market Overview

Current Market Landscape

  • EU defense market valued at over €200 billion annually
  • Defense spending increasing across member states, targeting 2% of GDP by 2030
  • €800 billion in funding through the ReArm Europe Plan/Readiness 2030
  • Growing focus on technological sovereignty and reduced dependence on non-EU suppliers

Strategic Shifts

  • Increasing emphasis on unmanned and autonomous systems
  • 15-20% annual growth in military drone segment
  • Prioritization of intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance (ISR) capabilities
  • Demand for smaller, more versatile platforms for tactical deployment

Market Gap

Limited EU-based production of advanced micro drones with specialized sensors, creating an opportunity to establish European leadership in next-generation drone technology.

Technology & Capabilities

Micro Drone Capabilities

  • Ultra-compact aerial platforms (under 250g)
  • Extended flight time (30-45 minutes)
  • Low acoustic and visual signature
  • High maneuverability in confined spaces
  • Encrypted communications and secure data transmission

LiDAR Integration Advantages

  • Precise 3D mapping and terrain analysis
  • All-weather, day/night operational capability
  • Centimeter-level measurement accuracy
  • Object detection and classification
  • Reduced reliance on GPS in contested environments

Technical Specifications

  • Weight: 150-250g (mission configurable)
  • Flight time: 30-45 minutes
  • Range: 2-5km with encrypted communications
  • LiDAR range: 5-100m effective scanning distance
  • Accuracy: ±2cm at 50m range

EU Defense Funding

ReArm Europe Plan/Readiness 2030

  • Ambitious defense package providing immediate financial levers to EU Member States
  • Total funding of €800 billion structured around key pillars
  • Focus on closing capability gaps and building a strong defense industrial base
  • Priority for collaborative European defense projects and joint procurement

Security Action for Europe (SAFE) Initiative

  • New EU financial instrument providing up to €150 billion in loans
  • Backed by EU budget with advantageous financing terms
  • Long-maturity loans (up to 45 years) with 10-year grace period
  • Pre-financing available (up to 15% of loans) as early as 2025

Strategic Fit with EU Priorities

This project directly addresses critical capability gaps in surveillance and reconnaissance while supporting European defense industrial base strengthening.

Manufacturing Location - Spain

Strategic Advantages of Spain

  • Strong aerospace and defense industrial base with 23,500+ direct jobs
  • Expertise in designing, developing, and integrating large systems
  • Significant player in European defense with established supply chains
  • Growing investment in R&D (6-10% of aerospace sector revenue)

Key Manufacturing Regions

  • Malaga (Andalusia): Home to MADES with expertise in high-reliability electronic systems
  • Madrid Region: Airbus' third-largest site and national headquarters
  • Aragon Region: Growing aerospace cluster with focus on drone development

Production Facility

Repurposing an existing electronics production line, reducing costs by 40-60% compared to building a new facility, with initial capacity of 1,000-2,000 units annually.
